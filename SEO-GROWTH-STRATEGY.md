## 1. Current State (Feb 2026 Audit)

### Site Inventory
- **Total indexed pages:** ~30
- **Blog posts:** 3
- **Landing pages:** ~20 (buying guides, category hubs, educational articles)
- **Interactive tools:** 0 live (ROI calculator mentioned in llms.txt but not built)

### What's Working
- Clean Next.js/Sanity architecture with good technical SEO foundations
- Strong on-page SEO (title tags, H1s, keyword placement)
- "How to Work Aged Leads" blog post is comprehensive (3,500+ words, scripts, templates)
- Multiple CTA types per page (AgedLeadStore affiliate, strategy calls, newsletter, free guide)
- AI-crawler friendly (llms.txt, llms-full.txt, explicit AI bot permissions)
- JSON-LD structured data (6 schema types)

### What's Not Working
- **Thin content volume.** 30 pages vs. competitors with 50+. Not enough surface area.
- **Category pages are hubs, not pillar content.** `/buying-leads` is a link grid, not 2,000+ words of rankable content.
- **Missing entire verticals.** No pages for final expense, medicare, auto insurance, mortgage protection, annuity leads.
- **Only 3 blog posts.** Competitors publish weekly.
- **No dedicated "how to work" content by vertical.** Your differentiator is execution strategy, but you only have one generic post.
- **Weak internal linking.** Blog posts don't link aggressively to buying guides and vice versa.
- **No FAQ schema** on most pages.
- **No interactive tools live** (ROI calculator, compliance checklist).

## 3. Strategic Pillars

### Pillar A: Vertical Expansion (New "Buy" Pages)
Add buying guide pages for every insurance/mortgage sub-vertical. Each follows the existing template but targets a specific product type.

### Pillar B: "How to Work" Content (Your Differentiator)
Create industry-specific execution guides. This is where you beat AgedLeadStore — they sell leads, you teach people how to convert them.

### Pillar C: Scripts & Templates Library
Dedicated pages for phone scripts, voicemail scripts, email templates, SMS templates. These are high-intent, high-bookmark, high-share content.

### Pillar D: Pillar Page Upgrades
Transform thin category hub pages into substantive, rankable pillar content.

### Pillar E: Tools & Calculators
Build interactive tools (ROI calculator, compliance checklist) that earn backlinks and repeat visits.

## 6. Technical SEO Checklist

### Immediate (Do with Phase 1)

- [ ] **Canonical URL audit.** Confirm `www.howtoworkleads.com` is the canonical and non-www redirects properly. Check all pages have explicit canonical tags.
- [ ] **FAQ schema expansion.** Add `FAQPage` JSON-LD to all buying guide pages and blog posts that contain Q&A content.
- [ ] **Internal linking audit.** Every new page must link to 3-5 related existing pages. Every existing page that's topically related should link back.
- [ ] **Meta description audit.** Ensure every existing page has a unique, keyword-rich meta description with a CTA.
- [ ] **Image alt text audit.** Check all images have descriptive alt text including relevant keywords.
- [ ] **www vs non-www consistency.** Sitemap references `www.howtoworkleads.com` — confirm redirect works both ways.

### Medium-Term (Phase 2-3)

- [ ] **Build `/aged-leads` hub page** as a new URL structure (or redirect strategy from `/buying-leads/buy-aged-leads`).
- [ ] **Implement HowTo schema** on all "how to work" guide pages.
- [ ] **Add `lastModified` dates** visibly on all content pages (E-E-A-T signal).
- [ ] **Expand author bio** with credentials, social links, and Person schema.
- [ ] **Create a `/tools` section** in the URL structure for interactive tools.
- [ ] **Submit new pages to Google Search Console** for faster indexing after publish.
- [ ] **Set up Bing Webmaster Tools** if not already done (ChatGPT uses Bing results).

### Ongoing

- [ ] **Monthly indexing check.** Verify all new pages appear in Google index via `site:howtoworkleads.com`.
- [ ] **Quarterly Core Web Vitals check.** Maintain 90+ Lighthouse scores as content grows.
- [ ] **Update `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt`** whenever new pillar content is added.

## 7. Internal Linking Strategy

### Link Architecture Rules

1. **Every buying guide page** (`/buying-leads/buy-*`) links to:
   - The `/buying-leads` pillar page
   - The corresponding "how to work" blog post for that vertical
   - At least 2 other related buying guide pages
   - The `/lead-order` page

2. **Every "how to work" blog post** links to:
   - The `/buying-leads` pillar page
   - The specific buying guide for that vertical
   - The scripts/templates post
   - The follow-up cadence post
   - The CRM guide post

3. **Every scripts/templates post** links to:
   - All "how to work" vertical guides
   - The follow-up cadence post
   - Relevant buying guide pages

4. **Pillar/category pages** link to:
   - Every child page in their cluster
   - Key blog posts in their topic area
   - Other pillar pages (cross-cluster linking)

### Anchor Text Guidelines
- Use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text (not "click here")
- Vary anchor text — don't use the exact same keyword phrase every time
- Natural placement within body content, not just in "related articles" sections

## 8. Link Building Strategy

### Tier 1: AgedLeadStore Cross-Linking (Easiest)
- **Ask Troy/ALS team** to link from their blog posts to your "how to work" guides
- Pitch: "We send you affiliate traffic, you send us educational traffic — mutual benefit"
- Target: 5-10 links from ALS blog to HTWL content
- This alone could significantly boost rankings

### Tier 2: Industry Community Participation
- **Reddit:** r/InsuranceAgent, r/MortgageBrokers, r/Sales — share guides when relevant (don't spam)
- **Facebook Groups:** Insurance agent groups, mortgage LO groups — same approach
- **LinkedIn:** Share each new post, tag industry contacts, encourage shares
- **Quora / forums:** Answer aged lead questions, link to guides as resources

### Tier 3: Guest Content & PR
- **Insurance industry blogs:** Pitch guest posts about lead conversion strategies
- **Mortgage industry publications:** NMP, HousingWire contributor opportunities
- **Podcast appearances:** Insurance sales podcasts (e.g., Insurance Agent Podcast, The Modern Insurance Agent)
- **HARO / Connectively / Quoted:** Respond to journalist queries about lead gen, sales, CRM

### Tier 4: Content That Earns Links Naturally
- **Original data content:** Aged lead conversion rate benchmarks, pricing surveys
- **Interactive tools:** ROI calculator (tools get linked to frequently)
- **Comparison content:** Vendor comparisons, "vs" posts
- **Templates/resources:** Downloadable scripts, checklists (link-worthy formats)

## 9. Content Publishing Workflow

### For Each New Piece

1. **Select brief** from `/content-briefs/` directory
2. **Research & write** content following the brief outline
3. **Create in Sanity CMS** — use the appropriate document type (landingPage or blogPost)
4. **Add structured data** — FAQ schema, Article/HowTo schema as appropriate
5. **Internal link audit** — add links TO this page from existing related pages, and FROM this page to related content
6. **Publish & verify** — check page renders, meta tags correct, schema validates
7. **Submit to GSC** — request indexing in Google Search Console
8. **Promote** — share on LinkedIn, newsletter mention where relevant
9. **Mark brief as complete** — update status in this doc
