### H2: Case Study 1 — Solo Life Insurance Agent
- **Situation:** New agent, $500 marketing budget, no existing book of business
- **Approach:** Bought 1,000 aged life insurance leads (90-day old) for $2 each
- **System:** GoHighLevel, 7-day cadence, scripts from [link to scripts post]
- **Results:** 15% contact rate → 22 appointments → 8 policies → $4,800 in first-year commission
- **ROI:** $2,000 invested → $4,800 revenue = 140% ROI
- **Key lesson:** Starting with aged leads let him practice without the pressure of expensive fresh leads

### H2: Case Study 2 — Mortgage Loan Officer
- **Situation:** Experienced LO, rates rising, pipeline thinning
- **Approach:** Bought 2,000 aged mortgage leads (60-180 day) for $3 each
- **System:** Close CRM, rate-drop trigger email, personalized follow-up
- **Results:** 20% contact rate → 80 conversations → 12 applications → 6 closed loans
- **ROI:** $6,000 invested → $18,000 in commissions = 200% ROI
- **Key lesson:** Aged mortgage leads contain people who couldn't qualify before — rate changes and credit improvements reactivate them

### H2: Case Study 3 — Final Expense Insurance Agency (3 Reps)
- **Situation:** Small agency, reps running out of leads, needed consistent pipeline
- **Approach:** 3,000 aged FE leads per month, distributed across 3 reps
- **System:** GoHighLevel, telesales cadence, daily calling blocks
- **Results:** Across the team: 2.5% close rate → 75 policies/month → ~$30,000/month revenue
- **ROI:** $4,500/month investment → $30,000/month revenue = 567% ROI
- **Key lesson:** Aged leads at scale create predictable, repeatable revenue for teams

### H2: Case Study 4 — Solar Sales Rep
- **Situation:** Solar installer in Arizona, seasonal slowdown in winter
- **Approach:** 500 aged solar leads (180-day) for $1.50 each during off-season
- **System:** Pre-qualified by running addresses through satellite tool, then called qualified subset
- **Results:** 40% pre-qualified → called 200 → 25 site visits → 8 installs
- **ROI:** $750 invested → $16,000 in commissions = 2,033% ROI
- **Key lesson:** Pre-qualifying aged solar leads by address dramatically improves conversion

### H2: Case Study 5 — Health Insurance Agent (ACA Enrollment)
- **Situation:** Health agent preparing for ACA Open Enrollment
- **Approach:** Bought 5,000 aged health leads (120-day) 2 months before OEP
- **System:** Email drip campaign for 8 weeks, then phone blitz during enrollment
- **Results:** Nurtured to enrollment → 400 contacted → 65 enrolled
- **ROI:** $5,000 invested → $19,500 in commissions (first year) = 290% ROI
- **Key lesson:** Buying aged leads BEFORE enrollment season and pre-nurturing via email creates a warm pipeline when enrollment opens

### H2: Common Themes Across All 5 Case Studies

2. They bought enough leads for a meaningful test (500+)
3. They followed a multi-channel cadence
4. They tracked metrics and adjusted

## Writing Notes

- **"Composite/anonymized" disclaimer is important.** Be transparent that these are representative examples, not named client testimonials.
- **The ROI numbers are the hook.** Lead with the results in each case study, then explain the system.
- **Make each case study follow the same structure:** Situation → Approach → System → Results → ROI → Key Lesson. Consistency aids readability.
- **Over time, replace composites with real testimonials.** As the site grows and agents share results, update with named case studies (with permission).
- **The "common themes" section is the real payoff.** It distills all 5 stories into the fundamental principles.
