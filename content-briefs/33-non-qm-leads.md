## Outline

### H1: Buy Non-QM Mortgage Leads: A Guide for Loan Officers in the Non-Traditional Lending Space

### H2: What Are Non-QM Mortgage Leads?
- Definition: borrowers who don't qualify for conventional/agency loans
- Who these borrowers are: self-employed, investors, foreign nationals, credit events, high DTI
- Why non-QM is the fastest-growing mortgage segment

### H2: Types of Non-QM Leads
- Bank statement leads (self-employed)
- DSCR leads (real estate investors)
- Asset depletion leads (retirees, high-net-worth)
- Foreign national leads
- Recent credit event leads (post-bankruptcy, post-foreclosure)

### H2: Non-QM Lead Pricing
- Fresh exclusive: $40-$100 (premium niche)
- Fresh shared: $20-$50
- Aged 30-90 days: $3-$8
- Aged 90-365 days: $1-$4
- Why non-QM leads cost more than conventional leads

### H2: Where to Buy Non-QM Leads
- AgedLeadStore (recommended for aged)
- Specialty non-QM lead providers
- Vendor evaluation for non-QM specifically

### H2: Why Non-QM Leads Convert Differently
- Higher revenue per closed loan (non-QM rates = higher LO comp)
- Longer sales cycle (more complex underwriting)
- Relationship-driven (borrower needs guidance, not just a rate quote)
- Repeat business potential (investors buy multiple properties)

### H2: Filters and Targeting for Non-QM Leads
- Self-employment indicator
- Property type (investment, 5+ units, mixed-use)
- Loan amount (non-QM skews higher)
- Geographic targeting
