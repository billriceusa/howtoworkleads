## Competitive Analysis

- Auto insurance lead content is dominated by carrier-specific advice, not independent agent lead-working strategy.
- No guide addresses the text-first approach that auto shoppers prefer (younger demographic, mobile-native).
- Multi-policy bundling is the key revenue multiplier for auto leads, but no competitor provides a framework for systematically upselling.
- Aged auto lead contact rates are among the highest in insurance because auto shoppers have ongoing need — policies renew every 6-12 months.
- Rate comparison positioning is underdeveloped — agents who lead with savings rather than coverage win more auto business.

2. **How fast do I need to respond to auto leads?** [Within 5 minutes for fresh, within 24 hours for aged — auto shoppers move fast]
