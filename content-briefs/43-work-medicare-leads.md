## Competitive Analysis

- Most Medicare lead content focuses on buying or generating leads, not working them through to enrollment.
- No guide addresses the full enrollment period calendar (AEP, OEP, SEP) with specific scripts and cadences for each window.
- T-65 (turning 65) leads vs. aging-in leads require fundamentally different positioning, but no one breaks this down.
- CMS compliance is a major differentiator — agents who violate marketing rules face penalties, but guides rarely cover this from a practical, script-level perspective.
- Medicare Advantage vs. Supplement positioning is the core product decision, yet no competitor provides a framework for guiding the prospect through this choice.
