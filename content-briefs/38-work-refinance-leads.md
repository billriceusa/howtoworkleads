## Outline

### H1: How to Work Refinance Mortgage Leads: Rate-Driven Strategies That Convert

### H2: Why Refinance Leads Require a Different Approach
- Rate-driven motivation vs. life-event motivation
- The borrower already has a mortgage — you're competing against inertia
- Savings must be tangible and immediate to overcome the "hassle factor"
- Rate environment determines everything

### H2: Understanding Refi Lead Motivation
- Rate-and-term: wants lower payment or shorter term
- Cash-out: needs equity for a specific purpose (renovation, debt consolidation, investment)
- How to identify which type you're dealing with in the first 60 seconds

### H2: The Rate Environment Playbook
- Falling rates: savings pitch — "Your rate is X, today's rate is Y, that's $Z per month in savings"
- Rising rates: cash-out focus — "Even though rates are up, your equity has grown significantly"
- Stable rates: term reduction — "Same payment but 10 years shorter"
- Script adjustments for each environment

### H2: The 7-Day Follow-Up Cadence for Refinance Leads
- Day-by-day breakdown adapted for refinance
- Day 1: Call + savings estimate offer
- Day 2: Email with rate comparison table
- Day 3: Call (different time) + text with personalized savings estimate
- Day 5: Email with "no-cost refi" or cash-out scenario
- Day 7: Call + breakup voicemail
- Extended nurture: rate alert emails when rates drop

### H2: Scripts for Refinance Leads
- Cold call opener (rate-drop scenario vs. cash-out scenario)
- Voicemail script
- Text message templates
- Email templates (savings-focused)
- Objection handling: "I just refinanced," "Not worth the closing costs," "I'm locked in at a good rate"

### H2: The Savings Calculation — Your #1 Conversion Tool
- How to quickly estimate monthly savings
- Break-even analysis: closing costs vs. monthly savings
- Presenting savings as an annual number (more impactful than monthly)
- "No-cost refinance" positioning

### H2: Working Aged Refinance Leads
- The dormant database play: rate drops reactivate your entire database
- How to segment aged refi leads by original rate
- The re-engagement campaign: "Rates have dropped since you last looked"
- Why aged refi leads appreciate in a declining rate environment

### H2: CRM Pipeline for Refinance Leads
- Pipeline stages: Lead → Contacted → Rate Quoted → Application → Processing → Closed
- Rate alert automation
- Break-even calculator integration

### H2: Conversion Benchmarks
- Contact rate by lead age
- Application rate from contacts
- Close rate from applications
- Revenue math at various loan balances and rate savings

### H2: FAQ
