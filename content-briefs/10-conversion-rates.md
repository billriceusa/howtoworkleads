## Outline

### H1: Aged Lead Conversion Rates: What to Really Expect (2026 Industry Benchmarks)

### H2: Why Conversion Rate Benchmarks Matter
- Without benchmarks, you can't tell if your system is working or your leads are bad
- Realistic expectations prevent premature quitting
- Benchmarks help you diagnose WHERE in the funnel you're losing deals

### H2: The Full Funnel — What We're Measuring
- **Contact rate:** % of leads you actually reach (phone, email, text)
- **Appointment/quote rate:** % of contacted leads who engage further
- **Close rate:** % of appointments that convert to sales
- **Revenue per lead:** total revenue / total leads purchased
- Each stage matters — you can have great close rates but terrible contact rates

### H2: What Drives Conversion Rate Up (and Down)
- **Lead quality factors:** data source, original form quality, how many times resold
- **Your system factors:** follow-up speed, cadence depth, script quality, CRM usage
- **External factors:** market conditions (rates, season, regulation)
- The 80/20 rule: your follow-up system accounts for 80% of variance, not lead quality

### H2: How to Improve Your Aged Lead Conversion Rates
- Quick list of levers to pull (with links to relevant guides)
- Improve contact rate: multi-channel, better timing, verified data
- Improve appointment rate: better scripts, value-first approach
- Improve close rate: qualification, objection handling, urgency
- Link to scripts post, cadence post, insurance guide

### H2: Red Flags — When Your Rates Mean Your Leads Are Bad
- Contact rate below 10% on 30-60 day leads = data quality issue
- 0% email delivery = bad email data
- High contact rate but zero appointments = wrong demographic or bad scripts
- When to switch vendors vs. fix your process

### H2: FAQ
1. **What's a good conversion rate for aged leads?** [1-4% overall close rate is healthy for most industries]
2. **Why is my contact rate so low?** [Likely data quality — check DNC scrubbing and phone verification]
3. **How long should I wait before judging aged lead conversion?** [30 days minimum, 60 days for mortgage]
4. **Do aged leads convert better by phone, email, or text?** [Phone has highest conversion, but multi-channel beats any single channel]
5. **How many leads do I need to measure conversion rate accurately?** [500+ for statistically meaningful data]
