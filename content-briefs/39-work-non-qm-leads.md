### H1: How to Work Non-QM Mortgage Leads: The Education-First Approach That Converts

### H2: Why Non-QM Leads Are Different from Every Other Mortgage Lead
- These borrowers have often been told "no" — they're skeptical and frustrated
- They may not know non-QM products exist
- Education is the conversion tool, not rate quotes
- Longer sales cycle but dramatically higher revenue per close

### H2: Understanding the Non-QM Borrower
- Self-employed business owners (bank statement loans)
- Real estate investors (DSCR loans)
- High-net-worth retirees (asset depletion)
- Recent credit events (bankruptcy, foreclosure)
- Foreign nationals
- How to quickly identify which non-QM product fits

### H2: The Education-First Framework
- Step 1: Discover their situation (why conventional doesn't work)
- Step 2: Explain the non-QM solution (product education)
- Step 3: Show the path forward (documentation requirements)
- Step 4: Remove the friction (guide through process)
- Why this approach works: you're solving a problem nobody else can

### H2: The 7-Day Follow-Up Cadence for Non-QM Leads
- Day-by-day breakdown adapted for non-QM
- Emphasis on educational content in emails
- Text messages focused on "I specialize in non-traditional lending"
- Call scripts that lead with empathy and expertise

### H2: Scripts for Non-QM Lead Types
- Bank statement lead script (self-employed focus)
- DSCR lead script (investor focus)
- Credit event lead script (empathy + solution focus)
- Asset depletion lead script (high-net-worth approach)
- Common objections: "I was denied," "I can't prove my income," "The rates are too high"

### H2: Documentation Guidance as a Sales Tool
- Walking borrowers through bank statement requirements
- Helping investors organize property documentation
- Making the process feel manageable, not overwhelming
- Why documentation guidance builds trust and closes deals

### H2: Working Aged Non-QM Leads
- These borrowers often need time to gather documentation
- The problem doesn't go away — self-employment, investment needs persist
- Re-engagement strategy: "I know you looked into this before — has anything changed?"
- Product updates as re-engagement hooks

### H2: CRM Pipeline for Non-QM Leads
- Pipeline stages with documentation milestones
- Automation: document checklist emails, follow-up triggers
- Longer nurture sequences (30-60 day drips)

- Contact rate, qualification rate, close rate by non-QM type
- Revenue math: 150-250bps on higher loan amounts
- Why one non-QM close often covers months of lead costs
