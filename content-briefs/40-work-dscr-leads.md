## Outline

### H1: How to Work DSCR Loan Leads: The Complete Guide for Investor-Focused LOs

### H2: Why DSCR Leads Require a Different Playbook
- Investors think in returns, not monthly payments
- They've often closed multiple properties — they're sophisticated
- Speed and competence matter more than relationship-building
- DSCR borrowers become repeat clients if you execute well

### H2: What DSCR Borrowers Actually Want
- Fast pre-qualification on the property (not personal income review)
- Clear explanation of DSCR calculation and minimum ratios
- Rate/leverage tradeoff options (75% vs 80% LTV impact on rate)
- Entity structuring guidance (LLC, trust)
- Portfolio scaling strategy (blanket loans, cross-collateral)

### H2: The 5 Qualifying Questions for DSCR Leads
- Property type (SFR, 2-4 unit, 5+, short-term rental)
- Purchase price and estimated rent (or actual lease)
- DSCR calculation walkthrough
- Credit score range
- Entity structure and experience level

### H2: Property Analysis Scripts
- How to run DSCR math on a call in under 2 minutes
- Rent estimation sources (Rentometer, Zillow Rent Zestimate, local comps)
- Insurance and tax estimation shortcuts
- Presenting the DSCR ratio and what it means for qualification

### H2: Rental Income Verification Deep Dive
- Existing lease vs. market rent appraisal (1007 form)
- Short-term rental income (AirDNA, 12-month average)
- Multi-unit rent rolls
- Vacancy factor adjustments lenders apply

### H2: Common Objections and How to Handle Them
- "The rate is too high compared to conventional"
- "I can qualify with my W-2 income"
- "I want to close in my personal name"
- "The DSCR doesn't quite hit 1.0"
- "I'm buying in a market I don't live in"

### H2: Follow-Up Cadence for Investor Leads
- Investors are deal-driven — follow up around deal flow, not emotion
- Day-by-day cadence adapted for investors
- Market intel as re-engagement hooks
- Portfolio review offers for aged leads

### H2: CRM Pipeline Stages for DSCR Leads
- Pipeline stages with investor-specific milestones
- Property-centric (not borrower-centric) tracking
- Automation: rate sheet updates, market alerts, portfolio reviews
- Multi-deal tracking per borrower

### H2: Conversion Benchmarks and Revenue Math
- Contact rate, qualification rate, close rate for DSCR leads
- Revenue per close: 150-200 bps on $300K-$800K
- Repeat business multiplier
- ROI model for aged DSCR leads

### H2: FAQ
1. **What DSCR ratio do most lenders require?** [1.0-1.25 minimum, varies by lender and LTV]
2. **Can DSCR loans be used for short-term rentals?** [Yes — many lenders allow AirBnB income with 12-month average]
3. **How do I handle a DSCR lead who wants to close in personal name?** [Explain LLC benefits but note most DSCR lenders allow either]
4. **What's the typical close timeline for a DSCR loan?** [21-30 days, faster than conventional investment property loans]
5. **How many DSCR leads should I buy monthly to build an investor pipeline?** [Start with 200-500 aged leads, scale based on contact rate and capacity]
