## Outline

### H1: Buy P&C Insurance Leads: Homeowners, Renters & Property Prospects

### H2: What Are P&C Insurance Leads?
- Property and casualty: homeowners, renters, condo, landlord, umbrella policies
- How generated: comparison shopping sites, home purchase triggers, renewal shopping
- Distinct from auto (separate page) but strong bundling opportunity

### H2: Types of P&C Leads
- **Homeowners insurance** — largest segment, triggered by home purchase or renewal shopping
- **Renters insurance** — lower premium but high volume, young demographic
- **Condo/HOA insurance** — niche but less competitive
- **Landlord/rental property** — investor-focused, higher premium
- **Umbrella insurance** — cross-sell from existing auto/home clients

### H2: P&C Lead Pricing
- Fresh homeowners: $8-$20 per lead
- Aged homeowners (30-90 days): $1-$3
- Aged homeowners (90-365 days): $0.50-$1.50
- Renters leads: $3-$10 fresh, $0.25-$1 aged
- Why P&C leads are affordable: shorter decision cycle, more volume

### H2: The Bundling Strategy — Why P&C Leads Are Gateway Policies
- Home + auto bundle = higher retention, higher LTV
- Renters → homeowners conversion path (nurture young clients)
- Umbrella as upsell on every P&C policy
- Multi-policy discount as selling hook
- Link to auto insurance leads page

### H2: Seasonal Patterns for P&C Leads
- Spring/summer: peak home buying = peak homeowners lead volume
- Fall: renewal shopping season
- Hurricane/storm season: triggers property insurance shopping in affected states
- Year-round: renters leads are consistent (apartment turnover is constant)
