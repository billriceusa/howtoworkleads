- High-ticket sale ($15K-$30K+) with long consideration period

- Why people delay going solar (cost, confusion, roof concerns, switching costs)

### H2: Qualifying Aged Solar Leads Before First Contact
- Pre-screen for: homeownership, geographic sun viability, roof condition (satellite tools)
- Utility rate check (higher rates = stronger pitch)
- Local incentive lookup (state/utility rebates change frequently)

- Adapted cadence for solar-specific touchpoints
