## Writing Notes

- **Tone:** Practical, zero-fluff. People come to this page to copy scripts, not read theory.
- **Format:** Make scripts EASY to copy. Use clearly formatted blocks with labels.
- **"Bad vs. Good" examples** are the hook. Lead with what NOT to say — it's engaging and educational.
- **Cover ALL channels.** This is the differentiator — no one else has phone + voicemail + email + text in one post.
- **Industry variations matter.** An insurance script is different from a mortgage script. Cover both.
- **This will be the most bookmarked/shared page on the site.** Template content gets saved and returned to.
