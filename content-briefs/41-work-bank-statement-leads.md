### H2: Qualifying Questions for Bank Statement Leads
- Business type and years in operation
- Monthly deposit volume (average)
- Personal vs. business account usage

### H2: Overcoming "I Already Tried" and Other Objections
- "I already tried and was denied" — reframe from conventional to alternative
- "My CPA says my tax returns won't work" — agree and pivot to deposits
- "The rates are too high" — cost of waiting vs. cost of premium
- "I need to talk to my accountant" — offer three-way call
- "Can I just add a co-borrower?" — explain when this helps and when it doesn't

- Call timing (avoid business hours for business owners)
- Educational content cadence
- Tax season considerations (Jan-Apr = high motivation)
- Re-engagement triggers (product updates, rate changes)

### H2: Conversion Benchmarks
- Contact rate, qualification rate, close rate for bank statement leads
- Revenue per close: 175-225 bps on $350K-$600K
- Why one close covers months of lead spend
- ROI comparison: bank statement vs. conventional aged leads

### H2: FAQ
1. **What's the minimum credit score for bank statement loans?** [Most lenders require 620-660, some go to 580 with compensating factors]
2. **Do lenders accept personal and business bank statements?** [Yes — some allow personal only, business only, or combined. Guidelines vary by lender.]
3. **How do lenders calculate income from bank deposits?** [Total deposits / months, minus expense factor (typically 50% for business, 0-10% for personal)]
4. **What's the biggest mistake LOs make with bank statement leads?** [Treating them like conventional leads — quoting rates before educating on the product]
5. **How long does it take to close a bank statement loan?** [45-60 days typically due to documentation gathering and alternative underwriting]
