## Outline

### H1: How to Work Aged Mortgage Leads: The Loan Officer's Complete Playbook

### H2: Why Aged Mortgage Leads Are Different from Insurance Leads
- Longer decision cycle (45-90 days to close a loan)
- Rate sensitivity — leads from a high-rate environment may be motivated when rates drop
- Life event triggers (move, refinance, equity needs) are time-specific
- Regulatory differences (TILA, RESPA, state licensing)

### H2: The Rate Environment Factor
- How to identify "rate-sensitive" aged leads
- When rates drop, aged refinance leads become gold
- When rates rise, purchase leads maintain value (people still need to move)
- Script adjustment based on current rate environment

### H2: Lead Types for Mortgage
- Purchase leads (new home buyers)
- Refinance leads (rate/term or cash-out)
- HELOC leads (equity access)
- Why each requires a different approach and script

### H2: The Mortgage Aged Lead Follow-Up Framework
- Adapt the 7-day cadence for mortgage specifics
- Day 1: Call + rate check offer
- Day 2: Email with current rate snapshot
- Day 3: Call at different time + text
- Day 5: Email with savings calculator or pre-approval offer
- Day 7: Call + breakup voicemail
- Extended: monthly rate update emails for long-term nurture

### H2: Scripts for Aged Mortgage Leads
- **Purchase lead script** — opening, qualifying (timeline, pre-approval, down payment), appointment close
- **Refinance lead script** — opening, rate comparison, savings pitch, application close
- **HELOC lead script** — opening, equity check, use-case discovery, application close
- Common objections: "I already got a loan," "Rates are too high," "I'm just looking"

### H2: CRM Setup for Mortgage Aged Leads
- Pipeline stages: New → Contacted → Pre-Qualified → Application → Processing → Closed
- Automation: rate drop alerts, birthday/anniversary drips, market update emails
- Recommended CRMs: GoHighLevel, Velocify, Encompass, Close

### H2: Compliance Considerations
- TCPA for mortgage marketing calls
- TILA/RESPA advertising rules
- State licensing (can you contact leads in other states?)
- DNC scrubbing requirements

### H2: Conversion Benchmarks for Mortgage Aged Leads
- Contact rate: 15-25% (30-90 day leads)
- Application rate: 3-8% of contacts
- Close rate: 30-50% of applications
- Overall: 1-3% of total aged leads → closed loan
- Revenue per lead calculation at various loan amounts

## Writing Notes

- **Speak to loan officers specifically.** Use mortgage industry language (pre-approval, LTV, DTI, rate lock).
- **Rate environment angle is the differentiator.** No one else covers how rate changes affect aged mortgage lead strategy.
- **One closed loan math:** If average loan = $300K and LO earns 100bps = $3,000 commission. 1,000 aged leads at $3 each = $3,000 investment. Close ONE loan = break even. Close two = 100% ROI. This math is compelling — lead with it.
