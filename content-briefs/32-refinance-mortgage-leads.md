### H2: The Rate Environment Factor — Why Timing Matters
- When rates drop: aged refi leads reactivate (the dormant asset thesis)
- When rates rise: refi volume drops but cash-out leads remain steady
- How to buy refi leads counter-cyclically (buy cheap when rates are high, work them when rates drop)

### H2: Rate-and-Term vs. Cash-Out Refinance Leads
- Different motivations, different urgency levels
- Cash-out leads are less rate-sensitive (project-driven, debt consolidation)
- How to identify and filter each type

- AgedLeadStore (recommended)

### H2: Aged vs. Fresh Refinance Leads
- Fresh: immediate intent but rate-locked to moment of inquiry
- Aged: lower cost, and rate drops make them MORE valuable over time
- The "dormant goldmine" — your aged refi database appreciates when rates decline

### H2: Filters That Matter for Refinance Leads
- Current rate vs. market rate spread
- Loan balance (higher balance = bigger savings = easier close)
- Property type and LTV
