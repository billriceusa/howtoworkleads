## Outline

### H1: How to Work Purchase Mortgage Leads: The Loan Officer's Playbook

### H2: Why Purchase Leads Need Their Own Playbook
- Different from refinance: life-event driven, not rate-driven
- The homebuying timeline creates natural urgency
- Competing with other LOs AND real estate agents for the borrower's attention
- Pre-approval is your opening weapon

### H2: The Purchase Lead Qualification Framework
- Timeline: when do they want to buy? (immediate, 3-6 months, just exploring)
- Pre-approval status: already pre-approved elsewhere?
- Agent status: working with a real estate agent?
- Down payment: how much have they saved?
- Credit: any concerns about qualifying?
- The 60-second qualification script

### H2: The 7-Day Follow-Up Cadence for Purchase Leads
- Day-by-day breakdown adapted for purchase mortgage
- Day 1: Call + pre-approval offer
- Day 2: Email with rate snapshot and pre-approval benefits
- Day 3: Call (different time) + text
- Day 5: Email with homebuying checklist or affordability calculator
- Day 7: Call + breakup voicemail
- Extended nurture: monthly market updates

### H2: Scripts for Purchase Mortgage Leads
- Cold call opener (aged lead)
- Voicemail script
- Text message templates
- Email templates
- Objection handling: "I already have a lender," "I'm just looking," "Rates are too high"

### H2: The Pre-Approval Advantage
- Why offering free pre-approval is the #1 conversion tool
- How to position pre-approval as value, not commitment
- Soft pull vs. hard pull — addressing borrower concerns
- Using pre-approval to lock in the relationship before they find an agent

### H2: Working Aged Purchase Leads vs. Fresh
- Aged: the timeline may have shifted — re-qualify the timeline first
- Fresh: speed matters — call within 5 minutes
- How to adjust your approach based on lead age

### H2: CRM Pipeline Stages for Purchase Leads
- Lead → Contacted → Pre-Qualified → Pre-Approved → Home Shopping → Under Contract → Processing → Closed
- Automation triggers at each stage
- When to add the real estate agent to the loop

### H2: Conversion Benchmarks
- Contact rate by lead age
- Pre-approval rate from contacts
- Contract rate from pre-approvals
- Close rate from contracts
- Revenue per closed purchase loan

### H2: FAQ
1. **What's the best opening line for a purchase mortgage lead?** [Offer a free pre-approval with a rate snapshot]
2. **How do I compete with online lenders for purchase leads?** [Speed, local expertise, and agent relationships]
3. **Should I work purchase leads from states I'm not licensed in?** [No — refer to a licensed LO and negotiate a referral fee]
4. **How many purchase leads should I work at once?** [Depends on capacity — 200-500 aged leads per month is manageable for one LO]
5. **What's the biggest mistake LOs make with purchase leads?** [Not following up enough — most LOs quit after 1-2 attempts]
