### H2: What Are Aged Leads?
- Clear definition: internet leads that are 30 days to 12+ months old
- How they're created: consumer fills out online form → lead sold to buyer → lead ages → resold at discount
- Why they exist: 70-80% of internet leads go unconverted by the first buyer
- Key stat: aged leads cost 80-95% less than fresh leads

### H2: Why Smart Sales Pros Buy Aged Leads
- **Cost advantage:** $0.50-$5 vs. $15-$75 for fresh
- **Volume advantage:** Buy 1,000 leads for the price of 20 fresh ones

- **Pipeline advantage:** Consistent lead flow without waiting for marketing
- **ROI advantage:** Even 1-2% conversion = strong ROI at aged lead prices
