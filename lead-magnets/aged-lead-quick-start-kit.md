## What Are Aged Leads?

Aged leads are prospects who submitted an inquiry — for insurance, a mortgage, solar, or another product — anywhere from 30 to 180+ days ago but were never contacted, never closed, or fell out of another agent's pipeline. They cost 80-95% less than real-time leads, and because most agents give up after one or two attempts, these prospects are still reachable and still have needs.

**Why they work:** The original problem (needing insurance, wanting a better rate, considering solar) rarely disappears. Life just got in the way. Your job is to show up at the right time with the right message.

## 3 Phone Scripts That Work

### Script 1: Insurance (Life, Health, Medicare)

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Company]. I'm reaching out because a few months back you looked into [life insurance / health coverage / Medicare options] online. I know that was a while ago — are you still looking for the right plan, or did you get that taken care of?"

**If yes:** "Great, what's been the biggest challenge in finding the right fit?"

**If no:** "No problem at all. Just so you know, rates have actually changed since then, and I might be able to find you something better than what you have. Would it be worth a quick 5-minute comparison?"

### Script 2: Mortgage (Purchase or Refinance)

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Company]. I'm calling because you inquired about [a home purchase / refinancing] a little while back. I know timing is everything with mortgages — are you still in the market, or did you already get that sorted out?"

**If still looking:** "Perfect. What's been the holdup — is it rates, finding the right home, or something else?"

**If already closed:** "Congrats! Just curious — are you happy with the rate you locked in? A lot of people I talk to find out they left money on the table."

### Script 3: Solar

> "Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Company]. You looked into solar panels for your home a few months ago — I wanted to check in because we have some new incentive programs that weren't available back then. Are you still thinking about going solar?"

**If yes:** "Awesome. The biggest thing that's changed is [mention current incentive — tax credit, local rebate, new financing]. Can I run a quick savings estimate for your home?"

**If no:** "Totally understand. Most homeowners I talk to didn't realize they could lock in [incentive] before it expires on [date]. Would it be worth seeing what you'd save?"

## Your 7-Day Follow-Up Schedule

This is the minimum cadence for every aged lead you purchase. Most agents quit after 1-2 attempts. You will not.

| Day | Action | Channel | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 1** | Call + Voicemail + Text | Phone + SMS | 10:00 AM local |
| **Day 2** | Email (value-first) | Email | 8:00 AM local |
| **Day 3** | Call (no VM) | Phone | 4:30 PM local |
| **Day 4** | Text message | SMS | 11:00 AM local |
| **Day 5** | Email (social proof) | Email | 9:00 AM local |
| **Day 6** | Call + Voicemail | Phone | 12:00 PM local |
| **Day 7** | Break-up email | Email | 8:00 AM local |

**Key principle:** Vary your timing and channel. If morning calls don't connect, try afternoon. If they don't answer the phone, they might respond to a text. The multi-touch approach is what separates top performers from everyone else.

**After Day 7:** Move to a long-term nurture sequence — one touch per week for 4 weeks, then monthly. Some of your best deals will close 30-60 days into the nurture.

## Top 5 Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake #1: Treating Aged Leads Like Fresh Leads
Aged leads don't need urgency pressure. They need relevance. Acknowledge the time gap. Say "I know this was a while ago" — it builds trust instantly because you're being honest.

### Mistake #2: Giving Up After One Call
The average aged lead converts between touch 5 and 12. One call and done is literally throwing money away. Follow the 7-day cadence above — every single time.

### Mistake #3: Not Filtering Before You Dial
Spend 10 minutes scrubbing your list before you start calling. Remove disconnected numbers, check for duplicates, and prioritize leads with complete contact info (name + phone + email). Your connect rate will jump 20-30%.

### Mistake #4: Leading With Your Product Instead of Their Problem
Don't open with "I'm calling about a great life insurance policy." Open with their situation: "You were looking into coverage a while back — did you ever get that handled?" Let them tell you what they need.

### Mistake #5: Buying Too Many Leads Too Fast
Start with 50-100 leads per week. Work every single one through the full 7-day cadence before buying more. Agents who buy 500 leads and cherry-pick the easy ones waste 80% of their investment. Volume comes after you've built the habit of consistent follow-up.

## Quick-Reference Numbers

- **Average connect rate on aged leads:** 8-15% (vs. 3-5% for cold calling)
- **Average conversion rate:** 2-5% of purchased leads (with proper follow-up)
- **Cost per acquisition:** Often 50-70% lower than real-time leads
- **Sweet spot for lead age:** 30-90 days old (best balance of cost and contact rate)
- **Touches needed to convert:** 5-12 on average
- **Best call times:** 10-11 AM and 4-5 PM local time, Tuesday through Thursday
