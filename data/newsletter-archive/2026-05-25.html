Quick Tips From the Trenches

Exclusive insights — only in the newsletter

The Rate Alert Response Script

When calling fresh mortgage leads, lead with rates before qualification: 'Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. I see you're shopping for a mortgage—great timing. Rates dropped 0.125% since yesterday, and I can lock that for you today. Do you have 90 seconds to see if you qualify?' This positions you as the rate expert, not just another loan officer.

Pre-Qualification by Phone: 4-Question Framework

Skip the 20-question interrogation. Ask these four in order: 1) Purchase price or loan amount? 2) Down payment available? 3) Current monthly income? 4) Any recent credit issues? These four questions determine 80% of loan viability and keep prospects engaged instead of hanging up on lengthy applications.

Beat Online Lenders With Local Advantage

When prospects mention Rocket or Better.com, respond: 'I understand the appeal of online lenders. Here's what they can't offer: I'm 10 minutes from your closing attorney, I know every appraiser in this market, and when issues come up at 4 PM on Friday, you get me, not a call center.' Position local knowledge as your competitive advantage.

Industry Insight

Summer Lead Volume Surge

Mortgage lead volumes typically increase 35-40% between Memorial Day and Labor Day as families time purchases around school schedules. However, conversion rates often drop during this period because LOs get overwhelmed. The solution: tighter qualification on the front end and faster follow-up cycles.
