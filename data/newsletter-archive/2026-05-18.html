The 5-3-1 Activity Rule for Lead Conversion

Top-performing lead buyers follow this daily minimum: 5 new lead contacts, 3 follow-up calls to previous leads, 1 appointment set. This baseline keeps your pipeline flowing even when contact rates drop. Track these numbers weekly — anything below this threshold predicts revenue problems 30 days out.

Stop Using Referral Language on Internet Leads

Never say 'Thanks for your interest' or 'I understand you're looking for...' on internet leads. These prospects often forgot they even submitted a form. Instead, lead with urgency: 'I'm calling about the mortgage application you started online. I have your pre-approval ready to review — do you have 3 minutes?' This positions you as the solution, not another salesperson chasing them.

The Low Contact Rate Motivation Framework

When contact rates hit 15-20%, use the 'Hidden Gold' mindset: every 5 leads you can't reach contains 1 that would buy if contacted. Your competitors are giving up after 2-3 attempts, leaving money on the table. Set your follow-up sequence to 12 touches over 90 days — persistence becomes your competitive advantage when others quit early.
