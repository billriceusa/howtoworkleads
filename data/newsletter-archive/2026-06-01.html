The 'I Didn't Fill That Out' Response Script

When prospects claim they never filled out a form, use this 3-part response: 1) 'I understand your concern' 2) 'You were researching [their need] online, correct?' 3) 'Great, I can help with that right now.' This acknowledges their objection while redirecting to their actual need. Works in 80% of cases.

Universal Appointment-Setting Close

After qualifying any lead, use this close: 'Based on what you've told me, I can see exactly how to help you. I have two options — Tuesday at 2pm or Thursday at 10am. Which works better?' The binary choice eliminates decision paralysis and the confident assumption moves them forward.

End-of-Call Summary Technique

Before hanging up, repeat their key details back: 'Just to confirm — you're looking for [solution] by [timeframe] and the main concern is [objection]. I'll have specific recommendations when we meet [day/time].' This confirmation reduces no-shows and positions you as organized and professional.
