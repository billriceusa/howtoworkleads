Quick Tips From the Trenches

Exclusive insights — only in the newsletter

The True Cost Spreadsheet Formula

Track these 5 columns: Lead Cost, Contact Rate, Appointment Rate, Close Rate, Average Sale Value. Your true cost per acquisition = (Lead Cost ÷ Contact Rate) ÷ (Appointment Rate × Close Rate). Most agents discover their 'cheap' leads cost 3x more per sale than premium ones.

Aged Lead Break-Even Analysis

Use this formula: If aged leads cost 90% less but convert at 30% the rate of fresh leads, you're still ahead by 170%. Example: $100 fresh lead at 3% close vs $10 aged lead at 1% close — the aged lead costs $1,000 per sale versus $3,333 per sale.

The 20% Discount Email Template

Subject: 'Volume commitment for Q2 rates.' Body: 'We're planning our Q2 lead spend and can commit to $X,XXX monthly if you can match [competitor's rate] or offer volume pricing. Can we discuss a partnership rate?' Send after 90+ days of consistent buying. Works because vendors prefer predictable revenue.
