Quick Tips From the Trenches

Exclusive insights — only in the newsletter

The 31-Day DNC Trigger

Set up automatic DNC re-scrubbing every 28 days, not 31. The 3-day buffer prevents accidental violations when your scrubbing service runs late. Use calendar triggers in your CRM to flag any lead approaching the 31-day mark.

Lead Velocity Scoring

Assign velocity scores based on response time: 5 minutes = 10 points, 1 hour = 7 points, 24 hours = 3 points, 72+ hours = 1 point. Multiply by conversion rate to get true lead value. Leads scoring below 21 points get moved to aged lead workflows.

Multi-Vendor Attribution Setup

Track lead source performance using UTM parameters with this format: utm_source=vendorname&utm_medium=leadtype&utm_campaign=monthyear. This creates clean attribution data across all your vendors without complex integration work.
