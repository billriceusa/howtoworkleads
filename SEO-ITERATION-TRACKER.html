Content re-aim + duplicate consolidation done · live

Root cause found: over-production on a DR-3 domain — 72/155 pages are <60 days old; ~36 pages have zero impressions in 90 days. The cron was manufacturing invisible pages (incl. 3 near-identical pipeline-management-framework* variants).

  • Cron throttled 3 → 1 post/week, re-weighted to the educational "how to work X" moat, depth over breadth, with an explicit ban on generic CRM/sales-theory terms we can't rank and a hardened anti-duplicate (slug-token) guard.
  • 5 confirmed duplicates 301'd into their canonical page (all 0-impression except the kept one) — kills cannibalization, concentrates authority.
